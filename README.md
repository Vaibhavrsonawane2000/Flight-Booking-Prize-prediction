# ✈️ Flight Price Optimization & Booking Behavior Analysis 

## Project Overview

This project analyzes flight booking data to uncover pricing patterns, airline performance, and customer booking behavior. The goal is to provide actionable insights that help users make cost-effective booking decisions and help businesses optimize pricing strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze how ticket prices vary with booking timing
* Identify the impact of stops, class, and airline on pricing
* Understand route-based pricing differences
* Provide data-driven booking recommendations

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* DAX (Measures & KPIs)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* Python (Exploratory Analysis - Google colab)

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards: Total Flights, Revenue, Avg Ticket Price, Avg Duration
* Airline Price Comparison
* Stops vs Price Analysis
* Route Matrix (Source → Destination)
* Departure Time Pricing Trends
* Days Left vs Price Trend (Key Insight)
* Class-wise Pricing Analysis
* Interactive Filters (Airline, City, Class, Stops)


## Key Insights

*  Ticket prices increase significantly as the departure date approaches
*  Non-stop flights are 20–40% more expensive than multi-stop flights
*  Business class fares are 2–3x higher than economy class
*  Certain airlines consistently follow premium pricing strategies
*  Flight prices vary based on departure time


## Business Recommendations

* Book tickets at least 2–3 weeks in advance to save cost
* Choose 1-stop flights for better price efficiency
* Airlines can optimize pricing based on demand timing
* Customers can use flexible timing to reduce travel costs
